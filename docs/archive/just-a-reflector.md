### Just a Reflector

<img style="width:100%;" sizing="cover" src="./images/project/just-a-reflector.webp" />

Just a Reflector is an interactive music video experience created by <a href="http://workshop.chromeexperiments.com/" target="_blank">Google Data Arts Team</a> and <a href="http://www.unit9.com/" target="_blank">Unit 9</a>. It takes advantage of WebGL technology to deliver real-time video effects in the browser. It was awarded <a href="http://m.emmys.com/news/press-releases/interactive-media-juried-award-winners-announced" target="_blank">Emmi Award</a> for Interactive Media Original Interactive Program.

My role in the project was to prototype and implement various interactive effects.

You can learn more <a href="http://www.justareflektor.com/tech" target="_blank">about the technology</a> and experience the <a href="http://www.justareflektor.com/" target="_blank">interactive video</a>. Non-interactive YouTube video below:
